### Common UX/SEO patterns to emulate (high level)

- **Above-the-fold clarity**: a single, specific H1 + 1–2 support lines + primary CTA.
- **Service discoverability**: service “tiles/cards” that deep-link to individual service pages.
- **Trust + proof**: stats, logos, reviews, and/or outcomes near the top.
- **Process section**: a concise timeline/steps (discovery → build → launch → optimize).
- **Scannable pages**: headings + bullet lists; minimal paragraphs.
- **On-page SEO basics**: unique title/meta description per page, clean internal linking, sitemap, robots, and structured data (Organization).
